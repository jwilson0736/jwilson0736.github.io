Mission Statement

To exchange knowledge between past and present to help our world grow and be a better place.

How this all got started.

A group of time traveler's got stuck in our time so we decided to use this as an opportunity to learn from them and also for us to show them things from our time.

What this site is for

This site is here so that people can make posts and inform others about the different times.

One must gain knowledge from the past to prepare for the future
